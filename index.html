Bets with CLV —
Closing Line Value (CLV) measures whether you got a better price than where the market settled. If you bet +150 and the line closed at +120, you got positive CLV — you found value before the books adjusted. Consistently positive CLV is the strongest indicator of a sharp bettor. Formula: CLV% = (close_prob − bet_prob) / bet_prob × 100
